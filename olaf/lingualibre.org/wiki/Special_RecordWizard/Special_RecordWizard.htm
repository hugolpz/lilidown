Here's how to do it:

  1. When you're ready, press the button above to start a record trial.
  2. Pronounce a word aloud, for example "the sun"
  3. Listen to your record to check that it's correct.
  4. Adapt your microphone's configuration if needed.
  5. If everything is correct, you can move on to the next step!
To learn how to configure your microphone, visit this help page.
